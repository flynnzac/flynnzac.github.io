  • GDP deflator note.

    Here is a revised explanation of the GDP deflator:

    To compute Real GDP, you use the prices from a certain base year: RealGDPcur=∑PbaseQcur.

    To compute Nominal GDP, you use the prices from the current year: NominalGDPcur=∑PcurQcur.

    To compute the GDP deflator, you use the Nominal GDP and the Real GDP from the current year: deflcur=NominalGDPcurRealGDPcur.
